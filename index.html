Gold Hunt Challenge

Rules

  • Game objective is to correctly choose the rock with gold behind it.
  • Users will have two tries per round to guess correctly.
  • Two incorrect guesses per round will result in one strike. Three Strikes will result in losing the game.
  • Levels get progressively harder from 1 to 10.
  • Correct Answer on first guess will earn the user 10 points, and on the second guess will earn the user five points.
